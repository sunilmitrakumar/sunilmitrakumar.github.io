Bio

I'm a development economist and Director of the King's India Institute at King's College London. I work on questions of causal inference in development using observational data, particularly in the context of discrimination and inequality involving caste, race and gender. I am an Associate Editor for the Journal of Development Studies and Economia Politica.

I received my PhD in Economics from the University of East Anglia, MA in Economics from the Delhi School of Economics, and BA (Hons.) in Mathematics from St. Stephen’s College, Delhi University. Before embarking on my PhD I worked for the Social Initiatives Group of ICICI Bank, an erstwhile major funder of non-profit activities in India.
